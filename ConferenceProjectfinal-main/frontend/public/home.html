2nd International Conference on Sustainable Materials Science, and Manufacturing ( ICSMSM 2K25)

The 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Materials Science, Structures, and Manufacturing (ICSMSM 2K25) will serve academicians, scholars, and professionals interested in mechanical engineering advances. ICSMSM-2K25 aims to enhance students, researchers, academicians, working professionals, and scientists. The conference will concentrate on technological and recent developments.
